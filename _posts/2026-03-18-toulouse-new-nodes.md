---
title: New nodes and board available on the Toulouse site
---

After some weeks of testing, we are very happy to announce that new nodes and boards are available on the Toulouse site :
* 4 [Qorvo DWM1001]({{ site.baseurl }}{% link docs/boards/qorvo-dwm1001.md %}) boards have been deployed in an anechoic chamber.
* 42 new [Qorvo DWM3001]({{ site.baseurl }}{% link docs/boards/qorvo-dwm3001.md %}) boards have been deployed in the same environment as the DWM1001 (38 in an office setting, and 4 in an anechoic chamber).

These new nodes are already available in our production environment, you can start experimenting with them as soon as you wish.

The positions of the new nodes are not yet known. A 3D LIDAR scan of the whole floor with sub-cm accuracy is scheduled in the coming weeks to establish a reliable ground truth for ranging, localization and time synchronization experiments.
